Murud Janjira

"A coastal fort that withstood centuries of siege and stands as a monument to resilience."

Murud Janjira is a historic coastal fort located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. The fort, built in the 15th century by the Siddis of Janjira, is renowned for its strength and strategic position on an island in the Arabian Sea. Despite several attempts by the Marathas and Portuguese to conquer it, the fort remains unconquered, making it a remarkable symbol of military defense.

The fort is accessible by boat and offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, making it a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Murud Janjira also boasts serene beaches and a vibrant fishing village, adding to its charm.
